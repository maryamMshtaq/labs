Ingredients

  • Hard Boil egg 1
  • Bread Slice 2
  • Cucumber 1
  • Lettuce leaf 1
  • Mayonnaise 1table spoon
  • Ketchep 1 tablespoon
  • Crushed black peper half teaspoon
  • Salt half teaspoon

Prepration

  1. Take a boiled egg and separate the yolk from the white egg part.
  2. Copped up the egg whites into smaller pieces.
  3. To make the egg mixture, mash the egg yolk in a bowl, then add mayonnaise, salt, and black pepper and mix well.
  4. Cut the cucumber into slices after peeling it.
  5. Remove the crusts from the bread slices and spread ketchup on both, then spread the egg mixture on one bread slice, top with cucumber slices and a lettuce leaf, and cover with the other bread slice.
  6. Sandwiches are ready after grilling on both sides.
